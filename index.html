Products

Design and Application

Different application and design requires different type of material and adhesive to make sure label adhere properly and look as designed.

Paper Based

General purpose, cost-effective label material. Widely use, from shipping labels, barcode labels, food and beverages, etc.

Plastic Based

Made from films/plastics, plastic-based label withstand chemical, oil, water, heat/cold and tearing better.

Security

Special material applied to secure product against counterfeit. Its commonly use for electronics void labels and warranty labels.

Hologram

Certain designs requires hologram to show depth and shine or even a proof of authenticity.
